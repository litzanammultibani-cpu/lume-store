# VAIYN — Packaging Suppliers

Compiled 16 April 2026. All prices in EUR. All suppliers have Trade Assurance + 4.5★+ rating.

---

## TL;DR

- **Phase 1 (samples only):** skip packaging entirely. Yilun's free hang tag + a handwritten thank-you note is enough.
- **Phase 2 (first 30 customer orders):** buy 500 A1 drawstring pouches (€50 total, covers ~500 orders) + simple thank-you cards. Add gift boxes in phase 3.
- **Phase 3 (post first 100 orders):** upgrade to branded envelope pouches + printed gift boxes + polishing cloths + shipping mailers.

## Additional Recommendations

### Polishing Cloths
- Can be added to a Yilun order (their standard polishing cloth is ~€0.20–0.30).
- Alternative: local French wholesaler (avoids separate shipment) or Amazon Business.
- Include 1 with every bracelet — differentiator vs. cheap competitors.

### Thank-You Cards
- **VistaPrint** (France): 100 branded business cards from €10. https://www.vistaprint.fr/
- **MOO** (premium feel): https://www.moo.com/fr/
- **Local printer** (Strasbourg): ask for recommendations at any copy shop.
- Target price: €0.15 per card printed in-country.

### Shipping Mailers
- **Sendcloud packaging section** (French-hosted, direct integration with carriers): https://www.sendcloud.com/fr/emballage/
- **Kraft bubble mailers** on Amazon FR: €0.30–0.50 each.
- **Branded poly mailers** from Alibaba: €0.10–0.25 (MOQ 200+).
- For jewelry, a small padded envelope is enough — no need for a rigid box unless you ship the gift box separately.

## Recommendation

**For the €80 sample budget:** Skip all packaging. Spend the budget on bracelet samples from Yilun.

**For Phase 2 (first real orders):**
1. Buy 500 A1 drawstring pouches (€50).
2. Buy 50 custom printed boxes (€15–20) — leaves 20 in reserve for returns/replacements.
3. Print 50 thank-you cards locally.
4. Add polishing cloths as Yilun order add-on.

Total Phase-2 packaging: **~€100 all-in**, stocks you for ~500 orders.
